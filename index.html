üåü What is a Natal Chart?

Your natal chart (also called a birth chart) is a cosmic snapshot of the sky at the exact moment and location you were born. It's like a celestial photograph that captures where each planet was positioned in the zodiac signs and houses.

Think of it as: Your soul's blueprint, a cosmic DNA test, or a map of your potential and personality traits written in the stars.

üé≠ What is Your Rising Sign (Ascendant)?

Your Rising Sign is the zodiac sign that was rising on the eastern horizon at your exact time and place of birth. It's the "mask" you wear when meeting new people and how others first perceive you.

Key Points:

‚Ä¢ It changes every 2 hours, making exact birth time crucial

‚Ä¢ Represents your outer personality and first impressions

‚Ä¢ Rules your physical appearance and natural demeanor

‚Ä¢ Acts as the "lens" through which you see the world

üè† How the Chart is Organized

Your natal chart is divided into three main components:

ü™ê Planets: The cosmic actors (what energies are active)

‚≠ê Signs: The personality flavors (how the energies express)
